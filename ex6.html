Exercise 6

Display list of temperature numbers in both Celcius and Fahrenheit from -10 to +40 at 5 degree increments.
The list should look like:
-10C -- 14F
-5 -- 23F
0C -- 32F
5C -- 41F
................
40C -- 104F

Temperature Conversion Formulae

Fahrenheit to Celcius

C = F - 32 x 5/9

Celcius to Fahrenheit

F = 9/5 x C + 32
